Under Estimation of Life


Life is an accomplishment in itself. Everybody is granted
a certain amount of time to accomplish their goals to fulfill
a desired purpose in life. Life is a piece of treasure humans
take for granted until they realize what they missed out on.
It's a huge opportunity not everyone gets to experience.
However, If you take advantage you will find yourself in a dream called reality.

Having a partner is like a business investment in your future and happiness. Spending time with the right people could make a great investment for your future and time. You both could experience great things and go through life together on a journey of your dreams. However, spending time with the wrong person could set you back and hold you back from your dreams, which could cause you to lose something you could never get back. Time.

Having a kid changes your life, You want them to be great better than you ever were. You want to give them the opportunity that you never encountered. Teach them things they can't learn anywhere but only what you can teach them. Help them learn about themselves to make them great human beings. Investing in your child making sure you get a great return but with love and great lesson to make them great all-around. The best investment that you could give them is your time, the more time you spend with them the more they will absorb and you can mold them to be great and better than you and could just add on your your legacy.
